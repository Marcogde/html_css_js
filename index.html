How to start with a Ethereum Wallet

Starting with Ethereum brings with it a few easily surmountable hurdles.
On this page I will show you how to:

  • Create an Ethereum wallet
  • Sending Ethereum
  • View your transactions on the blockchain.
Let us begin...

Visit:
Metamask Website

Download the extension for your Browser

Open the extension by clicking on the puzzle icon in your browser

Click on "Get Started" to start the application

Since you don't have a wallet yet, click on "Create new Wallet" to create your first wallet

Read the information displayed carefully and then click on "I Agree"

Create a secure password to protect your wallet from unauthorized access

Write your Secret Backup Phrase on a piece of paper, keep it in a safe place. Never take a picture or a digital copy of this phrase! Now click on "Next" >
